Roof Order — Support

Roof Order turns the measurements you take on the roof into a complete, supplier-ready material order for GAF, CertainTeed, Owens Corning, IKO, TAMKO and Atlas shingle systems.

Contact

Questions, bugs, or a product line you'd like added? Email bestroofingnow@gmail.com. We usually reply within 1–2 business days.

Common questions

How are quantities calculated? From each product's published coverage specs (bundles or linear feet per package) plus your waste factor. Always confirm final counts with your supplier and local code before ordering.

Squares or square feet? Either — toggle the unit at the top of the Roof size section.

Can I share or print the order? Yes — tap the share button to text, email, save to Files, or print a PDF.

My supplier's SKU covers a different amount. Coverage can vary by region. Email us and we'll adjust the spec.
