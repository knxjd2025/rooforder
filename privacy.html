Roof Order — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Roof Order ("the app") is published by Best Roofing Now LLC. The short version: the app collects no personal information.

Information we collect

None. Roof Order has no account and no login. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

How your data is handled

Everything you enter — measurements, product selections, and prices — stays on your device. The app makes no network connections, includes no analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not use Apple's Advertising Identifier or any cross-app tracking. Orders you share or print are handled entirely by iOS.

Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
